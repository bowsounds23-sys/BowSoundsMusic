Brandon Bowman

CEO/Founder of Bow Sounds & Entertainment

"If anything change the mood of the day, I'm sure music is one of them"

Brandon aka "B Smooth" born and raised in the heart of Chicago's South Side. He later moved to Rockford and became the visionary behind Bow Sounds & Entertainment, bringing his unique perspective and passion for music to every project undertaken. With a mixture of high energy and creative upbeat vibes, he aims to connect with a broad audience through Hip Hop, Pop, EDM styles.
